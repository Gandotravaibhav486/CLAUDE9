# Legal Contract Analyser

A tool that tells you in under a minute if a rental agreement can cost you money — and exactly how to fix it.

## Problem

Most people scan, not read, rental agreements.

- **4/5 Indians** sign contracts without fully understanding them
- **1/3 tenants** face deposit disputes

The core issue is length, hidden financial risk, and biased clauses buried in legal language. Users don't know:

- Where they can lose money
- Which clauses are unfair or illegal
- What to fix before signing

## Who It's For

| User | Goal |
|------|------|
| **Tenants** | Avoid deposit loss, hidden charges, unfair lock-ins |
| **Owners** | Ensure legally enforceable and balanced agreements |

## What It Does

- Analyses a rental agreement in under a minute
- Highlights unfair / high-risk clauses
- Gives a **Risk Score (0–100)** for the agreement
- Shows **Tenant vs Owner Bias (%)**
- Identifies top financial risks with **₹ impact**
- Suggests exact fixes and safer clause rewrites
